CARNAVAL DE BARRANQUILLA 2023

“Anyone who lives it, is who enjoys it”

The Carnival of Barranquilla (Spanish: Carnaval de Barranquilla) is one of Colombia's most important folkloric celebrations, and one of the biggest carnivals in the world. The carnival has traditions that date back to the 19th century.
